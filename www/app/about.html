DNA is an Ethereum DApp to disrupt the financial ratings industry and help fight against fraud.

The original DNA proof of concept was envisaged and then created by the B9lab team during the Thompson Reuters 2017 Hack the Valley Hackathon where it won second place. DNA is a decentralised expert market that enables analysts to create their own ratings agency and provide services directly to customers. It is built on Ethereum and uses Blockone ID to manage identities and the Thompson Reuters Oracle to help analyse data.
